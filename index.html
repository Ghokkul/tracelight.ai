How the studio works

From spark to standalone orbit

We're not an agency and not a fund. We're the founding team — engineering, design, and capital under one roof — for every venture we start.

/01

Spark

Six weeks of brutal validation. We prototype the riskiest assumption first, put it in front of real users, and kill anything that doesn't burn on its own.

/02

Orbit

Surviving concepts get a dedicated pod — senior engineers, a designer, and a product lead — shipping weekly until the venture finds paying gravity.

/03

Ignition

We spin the venture out with its own team, cap table, and roadmap. The studio keeps a stake; the sun keeps burning without us.
