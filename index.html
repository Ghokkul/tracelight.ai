// under the hood

How the agent works

/ingest

Universal ingestion

Connectors for syslog, CloudTrail, Okta, EDR, firewalls, and Kubernetes audit logs. Schema-on-read — no brittle parsers to maintain.

/baseline

Behavioral baselining

Learns normal per user, host, and service — login hours, access patterns, process trees. Deviations are scored, not signature-matched.

/investigate

Autonomous investigation

On anomaly, the agent chains queries across sources, builds the kill-chain timeline, and writes a narrative where every claim cites raw logs.

/respond

Human-in-the-loop response

Containment playbooks with blast-radius previews. Autonomy is opt-in, per playbook, as your team's trust grows.
